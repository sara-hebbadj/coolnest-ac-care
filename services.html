AC Inspection

A structured first visit when the cause is unclear.

AED 149Demo starting price
Book an inspection

An inspection is designed for symptoms that need assessment before cleaning, parts, or repair work can be recommended.

Common symptoms

  • AC is not cooling properly
  • Water is leaking
  • Unusual noise or vibration
  • Intermittent performance

What the concept includes

  • Customer symptom review
  • Accessible visual checks
  • Initial performance assessment
  • Recommended next step

Best suited to

  • Tenants with a new problem
  • Homeowners unsure what to book
  • Single or multiple units

Important note

This prototype does not claim to diagnose a fault online. The service recommendation only organises the request.

AC Cleaning

Routine care for fresher airflow and cleaner accessible components.

AED 189Demo starting price
Book AC cleaning

Routine AC cleaning is intended for regular household maintenance or minor dust and odour concerns where a deeper duct service has not yet been established as necessary.

Common symptoms

  • Dust around vents
  • Mild stale smell
  • Routine seasonal maintenance
  • Reduced airflow from buildup

What the concept includes

  • Filter and vent cleaning
  • Accessible indoor component cleaning
  • Basic airflow check
  • Service summary

Best suited to

  • Apartments and villas
  • Regular household maintenance
  • Families arranging recurring care

When to choose something else

Persistent odour across several rooms or visible duct concerns may need a separate assessment for duct cleaning.

Coil Cleaning

Focused cleaning when coil buildup may be affecting performance.

AED 279Demo starting price
Book coil cleaning

Coil cleaning is a more focused service concept for units where accessible coil surfaces may have accumulated dirt and need targeted attention.

Common symptoms

  • Slower cooling
  • Longer running cycles
  • Visible buildup on accessible coils
  • Higher electricity use

What the concept includes

  • Coil condition review
  • Targeted indoor or outdoor coil cleaning
  • Post-cleaning check
  • Maintenance guidance

Best suited to

  • Units with established buildup
  • Homes with performance concerns
  • Preventive maintenance visits

Scope note

Access, equipment type, and condition can change the final scope and price in a real service.

AC Duct Cleaning

A deeper concept for dust, odour, and airflow concerns across rooms.

AED 799Demo starting price
Request duct cleaning

Duct cleaning is positioned for broader indoor-air concerns that appear across multiple vents or rooms, especially in larger homes.

Common symptoms

  • Persistent dust from several vents
  • Odour across multiple rooms
  • Long-overdue duct maintenance
  • Uneven airflow concerns

What the concept includes

  • Scope and access review
  • Duct cleaning plan
  • Vent and grille attention
  • Completion summary

Best suited to

  • Villas and larger apartments
  • Homes with multi-room concerns
  • Landlords and holiday homes

Booking note

Customers should include the number of units, rooms, and any known access constraints.

Emergency AC Repair

Priority review for time-sensitive loss of cooling or active leaking.

AED 249Demo starting price
Request urgent support

This option is intended to flag an urgent request for faster review. It does not guarantee arrival time or confirm technician availability.

Common situations

  • Complete loss of cooling
  • Active water leaking
  • Urgent occupied-property issue
  • Time-sensitive landlord request

What the concept includes

  • Priority request flag
  • Symptom and safety review
  • Availability confirmation
  • Technician assignment update

Safety first

  • Turn off the unit if safe
  • Do not touch exposed wiring
  • Seek urgent help for sparks or smoke

Important note

A real provider must confirm emergency availability. This prototype demonstrates the workflow only.
